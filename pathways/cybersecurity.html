🛡️ Cybersecurity Pathway

Learn how systems are attacked, defended, monitored, and protected — using concepts aligned to CompTIA Security+ and real‑world IT roles.

1️⃣ What Is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity focuses on protecting systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, misuse, and attacks.

  • Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability (CIA)
  • Why security is needed in every organization
  • How cybersecurity fits into IT careers
Security+ Focus:
CIA Triad concepts appear constantly on the exam.

2️⃣ Threats, Attacks, and Social Engineering

Many attacks succeed not because systems fail, but because people are manipulated.

  • Malware types (virus, ransomware, trojan)
  • Phishing and social engineering
  • Password and credential attacks
Think Like an Attacker:
If exploiting a human is easier than exploiting software — attackers pick the human.

3️⃣ Defense, Controls, and Secure Design

Security works best when layered and designed into systems from the beginning.

  • Defense in depth
  • Least privilege
  • Authentication vs authorization

4️⃣ Incident Response & Operations

Security incidents will happen. Planning and response determine impact.

  • Preparation
  • Detection and analysis
  • Containment, eradication, recovery

5️⃣ Cybersecurity Careers

  • Security Analyst
  • SOC Analyst
  • Cybersecurity Technician
  • Penetration Tester (advanced)
Reality Check:
Most cybersecurity professionals begin in IT support or networking roles.
