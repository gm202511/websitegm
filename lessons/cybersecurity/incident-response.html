Incident Response

Incident Response (IR) is the structured process used by security teams to detect, contain, eliminate, and recover from cybersecurity incidents.

The goal is not panic, not speed, and not punishment. The goal is to limit damage, preserve evidence, restore service, and prevent repeat attacks.

The Incident Response Lifecycle

1. Preparation
Policies, tools, backups, access controls, and training.
2. Identification
Detect and confirm an incident is actually occurring.
3. Containment
Isolate affected systems to prevent spread.
4. Eradication
Remove malware, disable accounts, fix vulnerabilities.
5. Recovery
Restore systems, monitor closely, return to normal operation.
6. Lessons Learned
Document what happened and improve defenses.
