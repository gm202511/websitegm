Incident Response

An incident is any event that threatens the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of a system.

Incident Response is the structured process used to detect, contain, and recover from security incidents.

The goal is limit damage, restore systems, and prevent it from happening again.

The Incident Response Lifecycle

1. Preparation
Policies, backups, training, tools, and response plans.
2. Identification
Detect and confirm that an incident has occurred.
3. Containment
Stop the spread and limit damage.
4. Eradication
Remove the cause (malware, accounts, vulnerabilities).
5. Recovery
Restore systems and monitor for re‑infection.
6. Lessons Learned
Improve security to prevent future incidents.
